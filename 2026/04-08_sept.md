# A Model Can Help Itself: Reward-Free Self-Training for LLM Reasoning

* Authors: CUHK
* Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.18814
* Date: 2026-04-06

## Notes

* Self-evolving Post-Training (SePT), a post-training method that alternates between self-generation and training on
  self-generated responses
* Sample with T < 1, train with T = 1
* Online training, not offline frozen dataset
